[RAW LYRICS]
[Hook]
I trace the empty spaces where you used to lay
Craving the sweet surrender of our secret hideaway
Your silhouette is dancing in the midnight glow
Leaving me burning fast while the time moves slow

[Verse 1]
The candle flickers down to a single flame
I hear the autumn wind gently whisper your name
My fingertips remember the curve of your spine
Pouring out memories like a heavy glass of wine

[Pre-Chorus]
The tension is rising in the quiet of this room
I'm intoxicated by the ghost of your perfume
I need you closer than the air I breathe tonight
Let's cross the line and make everything right

[Chorus]
It's a bitter pillow and a cold, cold sheet
Without the steady rhythm of your heartbeat
I'm tossing in the shadows, longing for your taste
Pulling you into my arms with no time to waste

[Post-Chorus]
Oh, this cold sheet, aching for your heat
This bitter pillow, craving something sweet

[Verse 2]
I left the porch light shining right through the rain
Hoping you'd come driving down this lonely lane
I want to pull you close against my denim chest
And let our wandering souls forget the rest
